About


Universities are wasting a large portion of their allocated budget on energy unnecessarily. The smart classroom system can help address this issue. It is a business model that can detect and count the number of students entering and exiting the classroom using a sensor system and cameras. This system will help prevent electricity wastage by automating the process of turning off electrical devices when not in use, thereby reducing energy consumption, costs, and human resources.
